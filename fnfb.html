Not For Broadcast is a game where you control the television in a dystopian society. You decide what people think by editing your way, censoring what you want and shaping the stories of celebrities and many others, and deciding who the public prefers; the radical Advance or the vengeful Disrupt.

On the left is Advance, and on the right is Disrupt. Advance is a government that calls for equality all throughout the nation by enacting the Wealth and Assets Act, a law where the visibly wealthy have to give back a portion to the ones who need it, eradicating poverty, and more. Disrupt is a group fighting to bring back the old days, and only forms shortly before the Transition Centre, an act where you can 'unburden' your loved ones. It's up to you on who the public will think is good, and in the middle of all of that, you get to see more drama unfold and get used to the reporters on the National Nightly News. Of course, the choices are made in the game. If you're interested, you can get it or the demo. If you're not, just look at this image of the Mayor of Bumley!
